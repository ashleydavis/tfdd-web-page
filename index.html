Build habits and processes to deliver quality applications quickly and consistently.

The Feedback-Driven Developer teaches you how to build an efficient and effective development process that’s powered by continuous feedback from tests, clients, and your own assessments.

In this book you’ll find vital insights on how to:

  • ✓ Build efficient and effective iterative development processes
  • ✓ Integrate feedback loops into a full stack development workflow
  • ✓ Use responses from users and stakeholders to prioritize features and tasks
  • ✓ Incorporate feedback into prototyping and experimentation
  • ✓ Building the good habits of a good software developer

Reliable and regular feedback is the core of successful software development.

In The Feedback-Driven Developer, you'll learn how to establish the kind of development process that helps you eliminate wasted time and ensure what you're delivering is working, reliable and most importantly, valuable.

About the book

The Feedback-Driven Developer distills veteran coder Ashley Davis's 30 years of experience into a comprehensive approach to creating a rapid, quality-focused development process.

This entertaining and personal book introduces dozens of practical techniques for using feedback throughout the dev lifecycle. You’ll see each idea demonstrated hands-on as you explore a full-stack web application. You'll learn how to plan for change, effectively refactor, set up and automate tests, and ship your software to production.

As you go, you’ll also explore refactoring into desktop and mobile versions of your applications, expanding the reach of your software. Once your software is in production, Ashley reveals how you can ensure it runs reliably with vital techniques for debugging and triaging problems in live systems.
